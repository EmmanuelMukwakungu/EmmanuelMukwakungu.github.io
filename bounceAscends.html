Bounce Ascends

Story

Join the adventurous bouncy ball named ‘Bounce’ that is on a quest to collect an artifact called the Mask of the forgotten sun that grants users eternal power or a single wish. This artifact is found in dungeon called ‘The Tower of Trials’. Cursed by his evil twin sister, bounce has been on his quest of a year and a half trying to regain his humanity. Jealous of all his good deeds and how much people loved him, she turned him into something she hated the most—a bouncing ball. For over a year and a half, he traveled far and wide, searching for a way to regain his humanity, but his only hope lied in the mysterious Tower of Trials, a dungeon rumored to contain ‘The Mask of the Forgotten Sun’, a powerful artifact that grants eternal power—or a single wish. Armed with nothing but courage and a perfectly round body, Bounce bounces into danger in a last shot to reclaim his true self

Enemies

Take on a tower filled with skeleton enemies guarding each and every corner, as their objective is the prevent you from reaching the top. As weak as these enemies may seem, one wrong move around them and it will cost you!

Platforms

Use the platforms to your advantage and platform your way up to the top of the tower. Make to avoid the spike traps along the way.

Health

Make use of the hearts to regain your health when taken damage. these collectables are scattered throughout the tower so keep a careful eye for them all.

Main Goal

Bounce and Ascend your way through the mysterious Tower of trials to retrieve the most powerful artifact ever known
